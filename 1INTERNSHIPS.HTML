OUR TRAINING PROGRAMS

Comprehensive skill development for diverse career paths with real earning potential.

HAND-Y-MEN PROGRAM

Practical Survival & Craftsmanship Skills: This program focuses on essential survival skills and traditional craftsmanship that empower students to create, repair, and thrive in physical environments.

Carpentry & Woodworking

Learn to design, build, and finish wooden projects. Master the use of hand and power tools, understand wood properties, and create everything from simple joints to complex furniture.

Metalworking & Welding

Explore the art of shaping and joining metal. This module covers basic fabrication, cutting, grinding, and various welding techniques like arc and MIG welding for both functional and artistic creations.

Pottery & Ceramic Arts

Get your hands dirty and learn the ancient craft of pottery. From wheel throwing to hand-building and glazing, you'll create unique ceramic pieces and understand the science of firing clay.

Sustainable Farming Practices

Connect with the earth by learning the principles of organic and sustainable agriculture. This includes soil health, composting, permaculture design, and growing your own food.

Basic Construction & Repair

Gain confidence in fundamental construction skills. Learn about basic plumbing, electrical wiring, masonry, and how to safely and effectively repair common household issues.

Tool Maintenance & Safety

A craftsman is only as good as their tools. Learn how to properly care for, sharpen, and maintain a wide range of tools, with a strong emphasis on workshop safety protocols.

HAND-E-MEN PROGRAM

Digital Skills & Technology Training: This program prepares students for the digital economy with cutting-edge technology skills that are in high demand across all industries.

Web & Mobile App Development

Build for the modern web. Learn front-end (HTML, CSS, JS) and back-end technologies to create responsive websites and functional mobile applications from scratch.

Data Mining & Analytics

Turn raw data into actionable insights. Learn to collect, clean, and analyze large datasets using tools like Python and SQL to uncover trends and make informed decisions.

Computation Design

Merge code with creativity. Use algorithms and programming to generate complex visual designs, patterns, and 3D models for digital fabrication and art.

Electronics / Internet Of Things (IoT)

Bring physical objects to life. Learn to work with microcontrollers like Arduino and Raspberry Pi, build circuits, and create smart, internet-connected devices.

Cybersecurity Basics & AI Intro

Understand the fundamentals of digital defense and intelligent systems. This module covers network security principles, ethical hacking concepts, and an introduction to machine learning and AI.

Digital Marketing Strategies

Learn how to grow an online presence. Master the tools of the trade, including SEO, content marketing, social media management, and paid advertising campaigns.
